Task1: Usability Problems

The most important usability dimension for the app is arguably efficiency. Since this deals with money, it's most crucial that there be no erroneous aspects. The method has a huge learability issue. It's quite impossible to understand how to use the spreadsheet without explicit directions.

The spreadsheet itself has a couple of logical flaws. One such flaw is the lack of a "Trinity owes" column. The variables column is unclear, but appears to be what Neo owes duplicated. The first and second columns"Trinity paid for Joint / Joint card paid for Neo" and "Neo paid for Joint / Joint card paid for Trinity", respectively appear to serve the same purpose. They don't divide the costs well. The only columns that are well made are the "Trinity paid for Neo" and "Neo paid for Trinity" columns.

The issues affect all aspects of usability. The learnability issue can be fixed by making a clearer spreadsheet. This would entail making it such that all variables are absolutely necesssay. They'd probably include: "T paid for N", "N paid for T", "N owes T", "T owes N", "Joint to be split", "Joint, to be paid by T", "Joint to be paid by N". This allows for their joint accounts to have three different options; splitting the bill in half, Neo paying the bill in full(to be added to what Neo owes), and Trinity paying the bill(to be added to what Trinity owes). This update will allow for all usability issues to be fixed.
